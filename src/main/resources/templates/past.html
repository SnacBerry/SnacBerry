Our Journey:

2019: The Beginning

First 100 bags sold at Austin Farmers Market. Sarah's kitchen becomes the first SnacBerry factory.

2020: The Growth

Moved from home kitchen to commercial kitchen. Hired first 5 employees. Expanded to 10 stores across Texas.

2021: The Challenge

Navigated supply chain issues. Pivoted to direct-to-consumer shipping. Grew online sales by 300%.

2022: The Recognition

Voted "Best New Snack Brand" by Food & Wine Magazine. Featured in Forbes "30 Under 30" food category.

2023: The Expansion

Launched three new products. Partnered with organic berry farms in Oregon. Reached 50,000 monthly customers.

2024: Today

From 100 bags to 100,000 bags monthly. 25 employees. Shipping nationwide. Still berry-obsessed.
